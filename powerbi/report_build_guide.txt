Zension Power BI Report — Page-by-Page Visual Build Guide
==========================================================

File:     powerbi/zension.pbix
Measures: _KPIs table (28 DAX measures — use ONLY these, not raw column sums)
Data:     source_marts fct_* and dim_* tables

HOW TO READ THIS GUIDE — CARDS vs CHARTS (not a conflict)
-------------------------------------------------------
The same MEASURE can appear in more than one place if the VISUAL TYPE differs.

  Visual type          | What it shows                         | Executive | Detail pages
  ---------------------|---------------------------------------|-----------|-------------
  Card                 | One total number (no breakdown)       | YES       | YES
  Line chart over TIME | Trend by month/quarter                | YES       | YES (different metric)
  Bar / donut / table  | Breakdown by partner, program, SKU…   | NO        | YES (one page each)

Example — GMV (SAR):
  OK  on Page 1: Card showing total GMV + line chart of GMV by MONTH
  OK  on Page 2: Card showing total GMV + bar chart of GMV by PARTNER
  NOT on Page 1: Bar chart of GMV by partner (that belongs on Page 2 only)

Example — MRR (SAR):
  OK  on Page 1: Card + monthly trend line
  OK  on Page 3: Card + bar chart of MRR by PROGRAM
  NOT on Page 1: MRR by program bar chart

Rule: Executive = totals + time trends. Detail pages = breakdowns by dimension.

Build exactly 5 report pages below.

Global slicers (sync across pages where noted):
  - dim_channel_partners.partner_name   (multi-select; required for partner isolation)
  - dim_programs.program_name           (multi-select; pages 2–5)

================================================================================
PAGE 1 — Executive Summary (monthly rollup)
================================================================================
Audience:   Leadership — monthly review (requirements: monthly executive summary)
Questions:  High-level view of commercial, recurring, and cash health
Allowed:    6 KPI cards (totals) + 1 time-trend chart (by month)
Not allowed: Bar, donut, or table visuals that slice by partner, program,
             product, status, or company — those go on Pages 2–5.

SLICERS
  - dim_channel_partners.partner_name
  - Relative date on source_marts fct_orders.order_date (last 12 months)

KPI CARDS (row of 6 — Card visual, one measure each)
  1. GMV (SAR)
  2. MRR (SAR)
  3. Active Subscription Count
  4. Collected Revenue (SAR)
  5. Finance Variance (SAR)          ← reconciliation alert (Q7 headline)
  6. Churn Rate

TIME-TREND CHART (by month — NOT by partner)
  Visual:  Line chart (or combo if scales differ)
  Axis:    Month from source_marts fct_orders.order_date
  Values:  GMV (SAR)     ← total over time, line 1
           MRR (SAR)     ← total over time, line 2

DO NOT ADD breakdown charts on this page (use detail pages instead):
  - Bar chart: GMV or Order Count by partner or program     → Page 2
  - Donut: subscription status                               → Page 3
  - Bar/line: refunds, collected revenue by partner          → Page 4
  - Bar: verified customers by company                         → Page 5

================================================================================
MODEL & BUILD TIPS
================================================================================
1. Save zension.pbix after confirming all 28 measures exist in hidden _KPIs table.

2. Activate inactive relationships in Model view if program/customer slicers
   return blank charts:
     fct_orders.program_id       → dim_programs.program_id
     fct_orders.customer_id      → dim_customers.customer_id
     fct_subscriptions.program_id → dim_programs.program_id

3. Format: currency → #,0.00 SAR | rates → 0.0%

4. FNF programs: add page filter or flag dim_programs.is_fnf_program = false
   until business rules are signed off (design_brief.md).

5. MRR and active subscription definitions are baked into _KPIs measures
   (is_mrr_eligible, is_active_subscription) — do not override with raw sums.

6. Pre-order / Jarir-Axiom: use Pre-order Share on Page 2; do not count
   waiting-for-delivery subs in MRR (already excluded in measure logic).
